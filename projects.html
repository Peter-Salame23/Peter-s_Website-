Personal Mental Health Assistant

A privacy-first conversational agent designed for mental health support. It leverages a medical API to provide accurate, reliable information about medications, tracks wellness trends, and offers general, safe advice for managing stress and anxiety. The agent will never provide diagnoses, but can post suicide prevention hotline numbers and promptly refer users to professional help if a situation becomes critical.
