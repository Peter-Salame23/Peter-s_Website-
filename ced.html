CED Conferences

While each photo may show an award, the greatest honors were never made of metal or glass ; they were the friendships formed, the knowledge gained, and the unforgettable moments that made every experience truly golden.

McMUN (Mcgill) - 2025

Hosted by McGill University, McMUN was one of the most competitive MUNs I’ve attended. I honed my leadership skills, collaborated with passionate delegates, and focused on handling Gender inequality with inclusive and innovative approaches.

CIAC (Cornell University) - 2024

Hosted at one of the world’s top universities, CIAC tackled urgent global issues such as climate change, deforestation, and the endangerment of bees, creating a high-stakes environment for impactful debate.

NYUMUN (New York University) - 2022

NYUMUN transported us back in time through a Southeast Asian historical committee, where we explored impactful events in depth and engaged in dynamic debates on alternative implementations.
